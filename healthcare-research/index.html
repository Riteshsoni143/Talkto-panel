Healthcare Research

Certitude Research works with dynamic methodologies and frameworks tailored for healthcare market research and analytics solutions. Our proprietary healthcare digital panels provide multi-dimensional access to an extensive network including specialty physicians, healthcare professionals, allied health professionals, pharmacists, medical technologists, patients and caregivers. From our Panel 5% comprises of the healthcare unit with the help of which we have served a lot of our clients and have met their expectations.

Our in-depth healthcare profiling is an effective way to measure and evaluate.

Demographics: Patient and healthcare professional statistics such as age, specialty, experience level and practice setting.

Psychographics: Attitudes, beliefs, treatment preferences, and perceptions that influence clinical decisions and healthcare choices.

Behaviours: Prescribing patterns, treatment pathways, patient journeys, therapy adoption, and frequency of healthcare service utilization.

Pricing: Price sensitivity, reimbursement impact, payer influence, formulary access, and the role of discounts and promotional strategies.

Brand: Brand awareness, perception, positioning, and preference for your healthcare solutions compared to competing brands.

Trends: Emerging clinical practices, technology adoption, regulatory impact, and evolving patient and provider expectations.
